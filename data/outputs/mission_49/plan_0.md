# [MARKETING] SaaS Pricing Strategy Definition
## Execution Plan

**Objective:** Define 3-tier SaaS pricing architecture (Starter/Pro/Enterprise) for thelab.lat propuesta-app conversion  
**Scope:** Strategy + business rules only (no technical implementation)  
**Success Criteria:** Approved pricing document ready for engineering handoff

---

## Phase 1: Market Intelligence
**Task 1.1:** Audit 5 direct competitors' pricing pages  
- **Action:** Document tiers, price points, value metrics, and positioning  
- **Verify:** Competitor matrix spreadsheet with URLs, prices, and key differentiators

**Task 1.2:** Interview 3-5 current propuesta-app users  
- **Action:** Identify willingness-to-pay and feature-value perception  
- **Verify:** User research notes with quoted price sensitivity ranges

---

## Phase 2: Value Metric Definition
**Task 2.1:** Define primary value metric (what drives pricing)  
- **Action:** Choose between: proposals/month, users/seat, features, or revenue-linked  
- **Verify:** Decision doc with pros/cons and selected metric justification

**Task 2.2:** Map features to tiers (Starter/Pro/Enterprise)  
- **Action:** Create feature matrix: Must-have (Starter), Growth (Pro), Scale (Enterprise)  
- **Verify:** Feature-tier matrix with clear inclusion/exclusion rules

---

## Phase 3: Pricing Architecture
**Task 3.1:** Set price points and billing cadence  
- **Action:** Define monthly/annual prices per tier with discount logic  
- **Verify:** Pricing table with ARPU targets and LTV/CAC assumptions

**Task 3.2:** Design upgrade/downgrade rules  
- **Action:** Document proration logic, trial periods, and grandfathering policy  
- **Verify:** Business rules document covering edge cases (mid-cycle upgrades, downgrades, cancellations)

---

## Phase 4: Packaging & Positioning
**Task 4.1:** Create tier names and value propositions  
- **Action:** Write positioning statements for each tier (who it's for, key benefit)  
- **Verify:** 3-tier positioning doc with persona alignment

**Task 4.2:** Draft public-facing pricing page copy  
- **Action:** Write feature lists, CTAs, and FAQ addressing objections  
- **Verify:** Pricing page mockup/copy doc ready for design handoff

---

## Phase 5: Verification & Handoff
**Task 5.1:** Stakeholder review and approval  
- **Action:** Present strategy to leadership + sales team for validation  
- **Verify:** Approved PRD with sign-off from decision makers

**Task 5.2:** Create engineering handoff document  
- **Action:** Compile pricing rules, feature flags, and subscription logic requirements  
- **Verify:** `PRICING_IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md` delivered to engineering team
